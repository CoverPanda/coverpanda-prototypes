Attach rates — per launched franchisee

Each service is recommended by the agent at the exact moment it's needed (the cooldown sprint, pre-hiring, lease day). Rates below are launch-cohort attach — drag to stress-test.

🏛️ Formation

Entity + EIN + operating agreement. $500 filing (market: LegalZoom/Stripe Atlas $300–700) + $200/yr registered agent renewal → $700 yr-1, $200/yr after. High attach: the franchise agreement literally requires an entity, and the agent offers it during the cooldown when the candidate has nothing else to do.
Attach rate

🏦 Lending

Build-out financing sized to Item 7. ~$400K average loan (franchise build-outs $200–650K) × ~1.25% origination/referral (SBA-referral market: 1–2%) → $5,000 one-time per funded loan. Attach is capped by cash buyers — roughly a third of candidates finance through the recommended lender.
Attach rate

🛡️ Insurance

GL + property + workers' comp to brand spec. ~$10K average annual premium for a staffed brick-and-mortar unit × ~12% agency commission (P&C standard 10–15%) → $1,200/yr, recurring — renews every year the unit operates, and the agent already tracks the COI for compliance.
Attach rate

👥 Payroll

~$150/mo per location — base + per-employee pricing at 15–25 staff (Gusto/ADP run $40 base + $6–12/employee) → $1,800/yr, recurring. Offered the week before the first hiring event, when the franchisee must pick something.
Attach rate

📊 Accounting

~$300/mo per location — franchise bookkeeping market runs $250–500/mo; ours auto-generates the royalty-ready reports the franchisor demands monthly → $3,600/yr, recurring. The stickiest line: books start at lease signing and never leave.
